About the event


"VITB Gamers Asylum" is a LAN eSports event of the game title "VALORANT" Students can form teams of 5 and participate in the event and compete against each other for the top spot. Only the students of VIT BHOPAL UNIVERSITY who are attending AdVITya 2023 are allowed to participate.



We are glad to inform you that the Virtual Reality and Gaming Club of VIT Bhopal University is organizing VITB Gamers Asylum Season 3

Some Important Rules for the event -



All the players have to bring their own devices and peripherals (i.e. Laptop, keyboard, mouse, headphone, mousepad) for playing.

All the successfully registered players must bring their VIT BHOPAL ID CARDS to the venue or they will not be allowed to play.


The participants registering through the google forms will be receiving a confirmation call from the Student Coordinators and will be informed about the Registration Fee submission.


Be A Volunteer (Only for 2022 batch students)


As a Volunteer You will be working with the Esports Management team closely and all other core members of the club, if your work is exceptional, you may be considered for core team members yourself! An appreciation certificate will be provided for your efforts!
